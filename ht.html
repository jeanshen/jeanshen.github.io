Haitong Secruties

Corporate website design

About

Haitong is one of China’s largest investment bank, though it lacked a modern and international brand presence. They were looking to raise the brand’s digital presence globally.

Role

  • Business Requirements
  • UX/UI Design
  • Project Managment
  • Testing
  • CMS Training

1. Solutions

Understanding the complex landscape of China’s financial market along with the key competitive set to identify the points of differentiation for the client. These salient points were then developed into a creative concept and complex digital platform.

2. Process

2.1 Research & Structure Revamp

We review the old website and conducted UX researches to find the best practice in the market. Through research, we drafted a list of important sections we would like to improve in our future website design. To clear understand the website structure, we firstly drafted a site map based on the old website.

To have better understand of the business requirements, we scheduled several round conversation with the stakeholders in client's different departments as well. After that, we revamped the website structure, and created new sitemap. We use different colors to distinguish pages in different levels and used various labels to mark different types of pages.

2.2 Wireframe Design

We created wireframes for both desktop and mobile to display the basic structure of the website, and lay out the content and functionality throughout the site.

2.3 UI Design

Responsive design on desktop, tablet and mobile.

2.5 UAT & CMS Training

We supported as QA in UAT stage, and also created a CMS manual and provided 2 sessions of CMS training for different departments to manage the content on new website.

3 Outcomes

The updated look of the website represents the modern and global footprint that the business is taking. The website helps to reposition the brand in the international market as a modern, forward-thinking and credible financial institution.
