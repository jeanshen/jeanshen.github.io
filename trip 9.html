Trip 9

About

Trip 9 is a travel app for Chinese tourist who is traveling in Southeast Asia. It helps users find local retailers, bars, restaurants and other serviceshelps. Users are able to search by GPS to experience more local play, and check the shop address, map and reviews.

Role

  • Business Requirements

Onboarding

On landing page, users can choose which city in Southeast Asia they would like to explore. Once they choose the city, trip 9 provides a list of popular places regarding to different categories, e.g. bars, restaurtants, spas, etc for users to explore. Users also can view the list based on their instant location.

Search for fun

Users can search local experience by different categories. All local places is listed and sorted by the distance for users to review.

Explore more

Users can explore more in the hot places, which shows the most popuplar local plances. Users can check the details of each places, e.g. address, map, phone number and review.
