Macy's China

Cross-boarder eCommerce

About

The client is one of America’s largest department stores and had identified China as a market for international expansion. The client wanted to use an online-only market entry model that would allow them to test the market before investing in traditional physical retailing models.

Role

  • Business Requirements
  • Art Direction
  • UX/UI Design

Process

1. Discover & Define

1.1 Research

In this stage, we conducted researches of the eCommerce landscape in China, and found that nowadays China eCommerce is moving towards content and social driven business. Content is blooming in China and changing the pattern of consumption. The crossover between content and eCommerce becomes new trend. For example, the video and editorial content is blooming with increasing amount of new media platforms. Meanwhile, social media has become a powerful channel for initiating online purchases in China. It is also the new service hub of brands and retailers for customers.

1.2 Sitemap & Wireframe

Based on the previous research, we figured out the localized UX solutions for China market.

For example, as client are going to bring the largest catalog into China, we enriched the product data and curated content to be relevant in the China market. In terms of product details, Chinese consumer prefer to review the rich content of product, so besides the base template which shows basic w2w info integrated from macys.com, we also proposed a rich template for hottest products, which will show the customized comprehensive info, including images and videos.
