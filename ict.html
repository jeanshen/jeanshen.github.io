Being her best self.

Insight: she’s looking for ways to shine and to inspire new lifestyles.

Solution: tell Macy’s about yourself and we’ll show you a tailor made experience. Macy’s will help her manage the stress of modern life in a personalised, lifestyle-driven way by giving her the tools to create her own style.

2 Design Iteration

She sees herself in xx

Insight: she wants a individualistic sense of style in a non-individualistic society. She sometimes feels uncomfortable sharing a photo of herself.

Solution: Macy’s must help her feel herself every step of the way through personalisation, gamification and intuitive AI. This is a key feature to drive loyalty. We call this the Macy’s star. Her Macy’s star will be an icon for progress and membership points.
