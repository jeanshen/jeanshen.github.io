About

A leading global provider of ICT aims to revamp the ERP system (DESKTOP/MOBILE) to better support the end-to-end management of the corporate marketing resources (for the promotion of the latest tech services and products), which including the Exhibition resources, Forum resources and SME (Subject Matters Expert) resources. The project vision is to better connect and serve the business clients by improving the efficiency of daily business operations on the system.

1. Discover & Define

In the discover & define phase, we conducted in-depth stakeholder interviews to empathize with the target personas. We deep-dived into business needs and scenarios through the interview and created personas, user journeys to state the pain points and come up with potential solutions.

  • Personas & Pain points

Key targets:

  • Account Executive

    • - Schedule client visiting itinerary of marketing resources
    - Initiate the visiting requests and fill in details
    - Send the orders (requests) to relevant parties and track the status

  • SME

    • - Check orders to view client visiting details;
    - Accept and track order status;
    - Prepare and complete the speech during the client visiting to share professional knowledge

Other targets:

  • Account Manager

    • Review and approve orders of client visiting

  • Duty Manager

    • Confirm the orders, assign orders to SME, track if the order is completed

  • Reception Manager

    • Review the reception

Pain points - Account Executive:

    Pre-research before order initiation:

  • Marketing resources is complex, and difficult to find.
  • There is no intuitive rating/reviews of resources and experts that can be referred.
  • Prefer to do pre-research work on mobile while out of office.

    During the order submission:

  • Too much information to manually fill in, and will start from the beginning if quit by accident.
  • The current status of order is not clear, there is no prompt for order completion.

Pain points - SME:

    Pre-research before order acceptance:

  • How to better understand the business client demands to get well prepared, and improve the customer experience while giving the speeches.
  • Because of business trips, it is not possible to respond in time.
  • How to quickly know overall details of the reception itinerary.

    After order confirmation:

  • How to quickly cancel or change the order at any time if any urgency.
  • How to evaluate our efforts, as there is no intuitive reviews of experts.

2. Ideate the solutions

During the ideation stage, we ran small-scale workshops to ideate potential solutions aligned with persona needs, and built user flows, wireframes to demonstrate the functional requirements.

Solutions for Account Executive:

  • Regarding the resource, all types will be well categorized for easier filtering, and more relevant resources can be provided in resource details to enrich users' knowledge.
  • The review/rating system can be developed to evaluate the work of all parties and it also can be leveraged for future reference.
  • Make the order process more visualized, easier for user to track the status.
  • Build mobile app to support the business.

Solutions for SME:

  • Provide basic resource information in the requests (orders), and SME can jump directly to the corresponding resource to view detailed information.
  • The review system can be developed to evaluate all experts' efforts and can help to improve their work.
  • Can cancel or change the order online, if there is a time limits.
  • Build mobile app to support the business.

3. Design & Prototype

Based on the solutions, we iterate our user interface design and high fidelity prototypes in this stage.

Marketing resources exploration

The marketing resources are well categorized and listed as Experience (Exhibition) resources, Forum resources and SME resources for ease exploaration.

Multi-dimensional information is provided to help Account Executive and SME view and understand the details.

Users can check the resource calendar to better schedule the client itinerary.

Account Executive: Order submission

To make the order status more clear, the process is visualized.

"Save for draft" and "Import" are provided to ease the process of order submission.

User can track the order status at any time after submission.

SME: Order acceptance & completion

SME can get notice of all relevant orders in time. After checking the order details, SME can confirm whether to accept the order or not according to the personal schedule.

After the order is received, the SME can continue to track the order until it is completed.
