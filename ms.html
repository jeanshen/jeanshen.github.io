About

The client is one of America’s largest department stores and had identified China as a market for international expansion. The client wanted to use an online-only market entry model that would allow them to test the market before investing in traditional physical retailing models. We helped client build the eCommerce website/App and provide customised digital experience for Chinese customer.

1. Discovery: Personas & user journeys

We started project with customer insights. The 200+ survey responses and 20+ interview records supported us to design the persona and user journey

The journey clearly shows how the target customers usually shopping on the digital devices. It helped us find the opportunities for our products

  • Young men/female

    • Age 20-30, Single, who follow the fashion trend and would like to get fashion recommendation from experts

  • Mid-age female

    • Age 30-40, Married, who spend most money on family products, e.g. Maternal and child, Household products, and have more interests on promotions.

2. Ideation: Workshop to gather business requirements

Product catalog

As client are going to bring the largest catalog into China, we enriched the product data and curated content to be relevant in the China market. In terms of product details, Chinese consumer prefer to review the rich content of product, so besides the base template which shows basic w2w info integrated from brand.com, we also proposed a rich template for hottest products, which will show the customized comprehensive info, including images and videos.
